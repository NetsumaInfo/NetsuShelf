# NetsuShelf Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

## Executive summary

Using NetsuShelf should not reduce your privacy compared with manually visiting a supported story page and saving that content yourself. NetsuShelf reads the page you choose, gathers the chapters and images needed for the export you request, builds the file locally in your browser, and saves it to your device.

The developer does not operate a NetsuShelf backend that receives your story content, chapter list, library, or generated export files.

## What NetsuShelf is for

NetsuShelf is a browser extension that converts supported web novels and story pages into offline reading exports such as EPUB, HTML, PDF, and TXT.

Its single purpose is to analyze a story page selected by the user, gather the requested chapters, and generate a local export with optional metadata and library management features.

## Information collection and use

NetsuShelf handles data only when you use the extension on a page that you choose.

This may include:

- The URL of the current page and linked chapter URLs on the selected site
- The text, metadata, and images needed to build the export
- Site cookies when they are required by the selected site to fetch chapters or images
- Local settings, parser preferences, and optional library entries saved by the user

NetsuShelf uses this information only to provide user-facing features such as:

- Analyzing the current story page
- Building an EPUB, HTML, PDF, or TXT export from selected chapters
- Saving the generated file to your device
- Saving optional preferences and local library entries in your browser
- Looking up optional metadata or grouping information requested by the user

NetsuShelf does not ask for your name, email address, physical address, payment information, or similar account identity data.

## URLs, parser settings, and local reading data

Some NetsuShelf features store small amounts of local data in the browser so the extension can work as expected the next time you use it.

Examples include:

- Parser settings tied to a site you chose to process with the default parser
- Optional reading list or local library entries that you explicitly saved
- Export-related settings such as naming preferences or output options

This data stays in the browser unless you remove it, clear extension storage, or uninstall the extension.

## Cookies

Some supported sites require browser cookies to load chapters or images correctly. In those cases, NetsuShelf may access the relevant site cookies only to complete the user-requested fetch from that site.

NetsuShelf does not use cookies for advertising, profiling, or unrelated tracking.

## Log data

NetsuShelf does not run a developer-controlled analytics or telemetry service for your story exports.

As with any browser extension, the browser and the visited websites may keep their own logs or records. NetsuShelf does not control those systems.

## Data sharing

NetsuShelf does not sell user data.

NetsuShelf does not use or transfer user data for advertising, profiling, or unrelated analytics.

NetsuShelf does not transfer your story content to a developer-controlled backend.

The only network transfers involved are the direct browser requests needed to load the pages, images, or optional metadata/reference sources that you explicitly request through the extension.

## Remote services contacted by the extension

Depending on the feature you use, NetsuShelf may connect directly from your browser to:

- The story site you selected
- Optional metadata or reference sources used by the feature you requested

Examples may include supported story sites and optional metadata/reference providers such as NovelUpdates, WuxiaWorld, Webnovel, or Baka-Tsuki.

## Data retention

Generated files are saved to your device through the browser download flow.

Locally stored settings, reading list entries, library data, and saved EPUB data remain in your browser until you delete them, clear extension storage, or uninstall the extension.

## Security

NetsuShelf relies on the browser's extension sandbox, extension storage, and the browser's own network stack. When personal or sensitive data is transmitted over the network, the transmission depends on the security provided by the remote site and the browser connection.

## Your choices

You can:

- Use NetsuShelf without saving anything to the optional library
- Remove saved reading list or library entries
- Clear local extension data by removing the extension or clearing extension storage

## Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated if NetsuShelf's data handling changes. The latest version will be published in the project repository or project wiki.
